About This Events

International Conference on Innovative Computing, Informatics and Advanced Communication Systems for Sustainable Development

The aim and scope of the conference focus on new emerging trends in Information technologies with innovative computing approaches under Artificial Intelligence/machine learning, IoT, Big data technologies, Data science Robotic vision computing, Fuzzy approach, Autonomous computing, and Urban Computing. ICICIAC-2022 fosters basic and applied research that can improve people's quality of life. The primary goal of conference to bring together scientists, developers, and practitioners working in the field of advanced computing from academia and industries.

Also to encourage the transfer of basic research into institutionalized and industrialized research, as well as the conversion of applied investigation into real-time applications. Beside, this conference will creates opportunities for collaborating at national and international level and networking among Indian and international institutes to promote research

All accepted papers will be indexed in Scopus.
